About Me

Who am i, exactly ?

I'm a curious and creative web developer with agency experience, having built websites for both well-known brands and local businesses. I'm used to work with tools like WordPress (Elementor) and developing custom themes on Shopify. I also contribute to open source and create npm packages when inspiration (or necessity) strikes.

I have a strong foundation in CSS, JavaScript and Node.js. I am passionate about web technologies and always crafting new projects to learn and improve my skills.

I am currently developing an E-Commerce CMS called Fathoms with Express.js, Liquid.js and MongoDB. It is inspired by the Shopify CMS and aims to be a simple and efficient solution for small businesses.

My philosophy

You may have noticed this page design is quite simple. I believe in the principle of less is more. That's my approach to web development and design.

I aim to create clean, minimalistic, and efficient websites that are easy to use and navigate. I prioritize the user experience, ensuring that my websites are not only visually appealing but also functional and user-friendly.

I also believe in the importance of collaboration and communication. I enjoy working with clients and team members to understand their needs and goals, and I strive to create websites that meet those needs.

About my stack

Like the design, you may have noticed that my stack is quite simple too. No React, no Vue, no Angular, no Svelte, no Next.js, no Nuxt.js.

I feel that these frameworks are often overkill for most projects, and I prefer to keep things simple. I believe that a simple stack allows for faster development, easier maintenance, and better performance overall.

PHP on the other hand, is a language I have no experience with. To be honest, it never appealed to me. But i have to admit that it is a great language for web development and i defenitely need to learn it. That's why i am actively looking for a job that will allow me to learn PHP and work with it.
